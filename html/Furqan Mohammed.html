About

I started actively using linux in 2016, with Ubuntu 17.04. I felt that it was a nice escape from Windows, and it offered a new sense of freedom. I got back into the fray with Debian Linux in 2017, and I haven't looked back since.

I enjoy working on challenging problems in code. If a problem makes me think about it, I'm definitly intrigued. I enjoy these challenges, since I know that I'll learn a ton throghout the process.

What I learn in turn, I share with whomever is willing to accept it - it's the reason why I also become a Computer Science Engineer. I enjoy not only learning new technologies, or new ways to approach problems, but I also enjoy sharing those new approaches, so that fellow developers and students can benefit.
